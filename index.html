Welcome to Merseyside!


Home of many great bands, clubs and people!

Merseyside is found in the North West of England, and is home to Liverpool, Wirral and St Helens to name a few. The iconic band The Beatles resided in Liverpool, along with many other celebrities, for example Cilla Black, Les Dennis, Tina Malone, Mohamed Salah, Robbie Fowler, Melanie C, The La's and many others.

Liverpool was named EU Capital of Culture in 2008, and it is clear to see why. It has an abundance of museums, art galleries, theatres and beautiful architecture across the city. Many of the city's pubs are housed in beautiful Victorian style buildings. The Philharmonic pub is definately worth a visit when coming to the city, due to it's beautiful architecture and fantastic food.

On the other side of the river Mersey is the coastal peninsula, The Wirral. Often labelled as the Paradice Peninsula (granted this is often by residents of the Wirral). The Wirral boasts stunning views of Wales on the south side of the peninsula.

Wirral residents will often take you to see their marsh in Parkgate. Odd to many from outside the Wirral, but the walk along the front in Parkgate, accompanied by a bag of fish and chips, or an ice cream is a standard way to spend a Bristish summer evening.

The Wirral also has other delights, such as beaches, a marine lake, and many country parks. If you are into walking, trekking to Hilbre Island is a great way to spend an afternoon. However, you will need to be aware of the tidal timetable, as the island is isolated twice a day due to the high tide.
